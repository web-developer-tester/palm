PEEYUSH MOHANTY

Peeyush Dhari Mohanty is a dynamic and strategic business leader on a mission to revolutionize the business landscape and propel it to new heights. VANDANA MOHANTY

The Director of Metro Group and the leader of The Presidency Hotels and Resorts and Metro TV Bureau, holds a management degree specializing in Finance and Accounts. Her guiding principle is to trust her instincts, as she believes that true wisdom emerges from within. For the past two decades, she has devoted herself to her passion for interior design, consistently enhancing the aesthetic appeal of all our projects. Vandana Mohanty is the creative force behind some of our most celebrated designs. Her deep interest in business is fuelled by her insatiable curiosity and her diverse travel experiences around the world. She endeavours to infuse the intangible aspects of her global adventures into her work. Vandana Mohanty champions the fearless spirit of today's female entrepreneurs, who fearlessly explore uncharted territories, making their mark with innovative ideas, managing thriving enterprises, and creating employment opportunities.

JYOTIRADITYA MOHANTY

The son of Peeyush and Vandana Mohanty, is poised to be the torchbearer of the Metro Group's future. As a graduate of BSc, he is currently undergoing a transformative journey to emerge as a successful developer in his own right. With the legacy of his parents as his foundation, Jyotiraditya's vision extends far and wide. His mission is to elevate the infrastructure of Odisha to meet international standards, contributing significantly to the region's growth and development. With determination and a commitment to excellence, he's set to leave an indelible mark on the landscape of both the Metro Group and Odisha as a whole.
