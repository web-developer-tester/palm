Metro Group is one of the pioneers & leading conglomerates of Real Estate, Hospitality, Retail, Infrastructure, Tours & Travels, Security Services and Agriculture. Founded in 1993, Metro Group acts as the stalwarts in the Real Estate industry, Odisha. Over the last three decades, the company has developed some of the finest residential and commercial complexes in Bhubaneswar, Cuttack and other well known urban housing settlements and colonies in Odisha.

Over the last 30 years, our staff has gained experience and our services have started to grab the market. With a neat-to-the-brim track record, timely execution and smooth coordination, we have earned a first-preference trust in the state. With that legacy intact, we are gearing up to launch Metro Palm International, the tower to enhance luxury, experiences and opportunities for Odisha. Expanding corporate spaces, pulling hospitality up a notch, adding glamour with showrooms and more. There's no looking back for us, until it is to laud our laurels and our legacy.
